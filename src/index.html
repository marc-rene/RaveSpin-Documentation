DJ in XR

Mix on the go with virtual decks. Put on a headset, grab your virtual DDJ-FLX4, and spin—anywhere.

RaveSpin lets DJs use XR (VR/AR) versions of their tools so they can practice and perform without hauling gear. Built with Godot 4 and OpenXR for Meta Quest.

Watch demo Tech stack

What you can do

Enough to mix for real: two decks, EQ, crossfader, beat FX, BPM sync, and an in-headset library.

  • Virtual controller

    A 3D replica of a Pioneer-style DDJ-FLX4. Use it in VR or in passthrough AR so you see your room.

  • Dual-deck mixing

    Load tracks on Deck 1 & 2, play/pause, seek, adjust tempo and pitch (~±10%), and sync one track's BPM to the other.

  • Beat FX

    FLX4-style beat effects: Delay, Echo, Reverb, Trans, Flanger, Phaser, Pitch, Crush, Compressor, Limiter, Band Pass, Panner, Stereo Enhance, Distortion. One active per channel (or route to both).

  • LibreBox UI

    In-headset menu: track selection, active deck cards, level meters, BPM spinner, and FX selection—all without leaving VR.

  • Track library & metadata

    Songs have title, artist, album, BPM, key, genre, waveform art, and notes. Browse and load from inside the app.

  • Passthrough AR

    On supported headsets (e.g. Quest), mix in your real space with the virtual decks overlaid.
