What is RaveSpin?

RaveSpin lets DJs use XR (VR/AR) versions of their gear so they can mix and perform on the go—without carrying decks or a laptop. Put on a compatible headset (e.g. Meta Quest), see a virtual DDJ-FLX4-style controller, load tracks from an in-headset library, and mix with two decks, EQ, crossfader, and beat FX.
