Features

A full breakdown of what RaveSpin does.

Virtual DJ controller (DDJ-FLX4 style)

In XR you interact with a 3D replica of a Pioneer-style two-deck controller. Use it in full VR or with passthrough AR so your room stays visible.

Dual-deck mixing

  • Two main decks (Track 1 & 2). Load track, play, pause, seek.
  • Tempo & pitch (~±10%). BPM sync one track to the other.
  • Crossfader to blend between channels.

EQ & sound colour

Per channel: trim, 3-band EQ (low/mid/high), lowpass/highpass sound colour FX. See Tech for bus layout.

Beat FX

FLX4-style: Delay, Echo, Reverb, Trans, Flanger, Phaser, Pitch, Crush, Compressor, Limiter, Band Pass, Panner, Stereo Enhance, Distortion. One per channel; choose which track(s) get FX from LibreBox.

LibreBox — in-headset UI

Main hub (active track cards, dB meter, BPM spinner), track selection, add track, FX selection. 2D UI in 3D for VR.

Track library & metadata

Song resource: title, artist(s), album, genres, waveform, BPM, key, origin, favourite, user note.

Passthrough AR

On supported headsets (e.g. Meta Quest), mix in your real space with virtual decks overlaid. Recenter on XR pose recenter.
